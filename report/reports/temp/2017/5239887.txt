My Republica [edited]<http://www.myrepublica.com/news/25224/>The District Public Health Office (DPHO), Chitwan [Province Three], has confirmed the death of woman from scrub typhus.The woman in her mid 20s of Rajahar of Nawalparasi district was admitted [Provinces Four and Five] to Community Hospital, Chitwan, on [5 Aug 2017], died on [6 Aug 2017] in course of treatment, said vector controller at the DPHO, Ram Kumar KC.Out of 109 people infected with the disease in last fiscal year, 4 died, according to data with the DPHO. Likewise, out of 264 people, who underwent scrub typhus test, till the date from [16 Jul 2017], 75 people were found infected with the disease.According to the Epidemiology and Disease Control Division (EDCD), the disease has been detected in more than 27 districts with more than 100 people infected with it.
