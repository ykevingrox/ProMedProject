Hepatitis E - France: platelet transfusion-related 20170115.4763642-------------------------------------------------------------------The case report of a severe infection with hepatitis E traced to a platelet transfusion is the kind of unusually severe infection which is the consequence of a hitherto unsuspected genetic immune defect. The high viral titers are consistent [with] such an event in this patient. With appropriate specimens and current methodologies, it is frequently possible to demonstrate such a mutation from the analysis of a single patient (1).1.
