Pakistan Today [edited]<https://www.pakistantoday.com.pk/2017/08/27/two-die-of-congo-virus-in-loralai/>Two people including a woman died of Crimean-Congo haemorrhagic fever (CCHF), commonly known as Congo virus, here on [Sat 26 Aug 2017].According to Health Department sources, BK, son of SY, from Chamzai, suffering from Congo virus, expired while being shifted to Quetta for treatment.Another woman in the locality also fell to the fatal Congo virus. Several people were hospitalized after being found affected with the virus. The locals have demanded the provincial government and health authorities take measures to prevent further spread of the disease.They alleged that the spraying carried out by the livestock department was not enough, and solid steps on a large scale should be taken against the outbreak.--Communicated by:ProMED-mail<promed@promedmail.org>[The above report does not clarify whether there was any contact history with cattle or ticks in the above cases.Balochistan province has been endemic for CCHF for many years, and sporadic cases are reported every year, particularly between May and September/October. - Mod.UBAA HealthMap/ProMED-mail map can be accessed at: <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/1299>.]
