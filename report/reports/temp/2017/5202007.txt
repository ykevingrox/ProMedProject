GIDEON (Global Infectious Disease & Epidemiology Network) [edited]As of 2017, Ross River disease and Barmah Forest disease continue to infect a considerable number of people in Queensland, Australia. In fact, local rates of these 2 mosquito-borne viral infections have changed little during the past 25 years.
