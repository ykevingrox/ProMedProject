The patient is a 40-year-old male from Sikander Khel bala, district Bannu, with onset of symptoms from [14 Sep 2017] and admitted at HMC on [29 Sep 2017] with fever, body aches, vomiting, and bleeding tendency (nose and gums). His initial platelet count was 65 000 at the time of admission. He had a history of animal contacts at home. His blood sample was collected for CCHF serology, which was reported positive for CCHF. All of his contacts including the health care providers have been noted.
