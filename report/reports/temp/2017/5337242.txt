EpiCore Global Surveillance Project [edited][Re: ProMED-mail Brucellosis - USA (12): (WY) possible elk exposure, RFI Archive Number: 20170923.5336298]The culture isolate was confirmed to be _Brucella abortus_. Genomic identification was recently conducted at the National Veterinary Services Laboratory in an attempt to link it to the most similar isolate in their database. It is believed this was from a WY exposure, not exotic (India).--Communicated by:EpiCore Global Surveillance Project<promed@promedmail.org>[ProMED thanks the EpiCore Global Surveillance Project member for the updated information. - Mod.LLA HealthMap/ProMED-mail map can be accessed at: <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/251>.]
