CDC [edited]<https://www.cdc.gov/ecoli/2017/o157h7-03-17/index.html>Since the last update on 21 Mar 2017, 6 more ill people have been reported from 4 states.29 people infected with the outbreak strains of EHEC O157:H7 have been reported from 12 states. A list of the states and the number of cases in each can be found on the Case Count Map page [see below].Illnesses started on dates ranging from 4 Jan 2017 to 13 Mar 2017. Ill people range in age from 1 to 57 years, with a median age of 8. 24 (83 percent) of the 29 ill people are younger than 18 years. Among ill people, 59 percent are male. 12 ill people have been hospitalized, and 9 people developed hemolytic uremic syndrome, a type of kidney failure. No deaths have been reported.Investigation Update--------------------------In interviews, ill people or their family members answered questions about the foods they ate and other exposures in the week before they became ill. 21 (75 percent) of the 28 people reached for interview reported either eating I.M. Healthy brand SoyNut Butter at home (15 people) in the week before they became ill, attending a facility that served I.M. Healthy brand SoyNut Butter (2 people), or attending childcare centers that served I.M. Healthy brand SoyNut Butter and I.M. Healthy brand granola coated with SoyNut Butter (4 people).The investigation is ongoing.
