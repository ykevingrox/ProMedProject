COLI EHEC - CANADA (04): O121, FLOUR (March 29, 2017):There are 25 cases of _E. coli_ O121 under investigation. A sample of Robin Hood flour was collected from one case's home. The sample tested positive for _E. coli_ O121 and had matching PFGE to the clinical cases. Subsequently, a sample of Robin Hood flour collected from a retail location also tested positive for _E. coli_ O121. Several cases reported contact with Robin Hood flour. The investigation is ongoing and it is possible that additional products linked to the outbreak investigation may be identified. Isolates from the 2016 US outbreak have been compared to the current outbreak in Canada by whole genome sequencing (via PulseNet International); the Canadian outbreak strain is not similar to the US outbreak. Comparisons will continue to be made on an ongoing basis throughout the outbreak investigation in Canada.--Communicated by:Public Health Agency of Canada[ProMED thanks the Public Health Agency of Canada for their quick response. - Mod.LLA HealthMap/ProMED-mail map can be accessed at: <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/12>.][See Also:E. coli EHEC - Canada (04): O121, flour  20170329.4933953E. coli EHEC - Canada (03): O121 20170323.4920524E. coli EHEC - Canada (02): O121, RFI 20170321.4915570E. coli EHEC - Canada: O121 20170116.47694662016----E. coli EHEC - USA (25): O121, O26, flour, alert, expanded recall 20160726.4371012E. coli EHEC - Canada: (AB) O157 20160204.3991821E. coli EHEC - USA (16): O121, flour, alert, recall 20160602.4261085E. coli EHEC - USA (14): O121, flour, alert, recall 20160601.42582222015----E. coli EHEC - Canada (04): O157 20150916.3647354E. coli EHEC - Canada (03): O157 20150826.3602244E. coli EHEC - Canada (02): (ON) O157, leafy greens susp, correction 20150421.3313162E. coli EHEC - Canada (02): (ON) O157, leafy greens susp 20150421.3312096E. coli EHEC - Canada: O157 20150416.33015672014----E. coli EHEC - Canada (07): (NU) RFI 20141217.3039697E. coli EHEC - Canada (06): (ON) O157, unpasteurized cider, alert, recall 20141101.2920416E. coli EHEC - Canada (05): (AB) O157, chicken sausage products, recall 20141005.2833262E. coli EHEC - Canada (04): (AB) O157, pork products, recall 20140909.2759887E. coli EHEC - Canada (03): (AB) 20140820.2707399E. coli EHEC - Canada (02): (AB) sprouts, O157 20140803.2655749E. coli EHEC - Canada: (QC) beef tartare, RFI 20140116.21754362013 ----E. coli EHEC - USA (33): O121 frozen snacks, flour source 20131117.20600292011 ----E. coli O157, 2009 - USA: cookie dough, raw flour source 20111217.3619].................................................ll/ec/jh"
