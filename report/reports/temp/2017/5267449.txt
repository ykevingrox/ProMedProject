EpiCore Global Surveillance Project [edited]According to the Mainichi Shimbun News (<https://mainichi.jp/articles/20170823/k00/00m/040/113000c> [in Japanese]), _Escherichia coli_ O157 was detected from a total of 10 cases. 6 consumed potato salad that was provided by a supermarket called the Delicious Kagohara. 3 consumed potato salad sold by the Delicious Kumagaya, a supermarket affiliated with Delicious Kagohara. Another case was reported from Maebashi city who consumed potato salad sold by another affiliated supermarket in Maebashi. The potato salad was produced by a food producer located in Takasaki city, Gumma prefecture. The detailed information on the ingredients that might have caused illness is not reported in the news. The 5 year-old girl who developed hemolytic-uremic syndrome (HUS) has [regained consciousness], but is still hospitalized.--communicated by:EpiCore Surveillance Project memberDr Masaki OtaResearch Institute of Tuberculosis TokyoJapan[ProMED-mail thanks Dr Ota for his swift contribution. - Mod.LLA HealthMap/ProMED-mail map can be accessed at: <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/5021>.]
