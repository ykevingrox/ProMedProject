The Times of India [edited]<http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/mumbai/1-dies-of-dengue-another-of-lepto-in-5-days-in-a-wet-city/articleshow/54663855.cms>The extended rain spell over the city has claimed 2 lives in the past 5 days, a health report released by the civic authorities late on Monday [3 Oct 2016] showed.A 24-year-old Kurla woman died on 29 Sep 2016 due to a brain infection along with leptospirosis.Leptospirosis is a bacterial infection that spreads through flood waters contaminated with the urine of an infected dog or rat. The woman, who lived in the Raghunath chawl of Naupada area, had given a history of wading through stagnant water.With these deaths, which occurred in BMC-run KEM Hospital in Parel, the BMC confirmed that leptospirosis has so far claimed 8 lives.
