Kaz Tag, The Kazakh Telegraph Agency [edited]<http://www.kaztag.kz/en/news/detail.php?ID=468646>A 10-year-old child is among people hospitalized with [suspected] anthrax said Aizhan Isa, spokeswoman of the health care department of Karaganda region. ""Inhabitants of Shet district aged 23-50 have been taken to the infection hospital since [20 Jun 2016]. Among them is a 10 years old child. They were hospitalized to exclude dangerous infections, including anthrax,"" she said. She said tests have been made.""Unfortunately 2 people died, a man and a woman. The man was about 50, the woman 35-40. They were hospitalized [and their condition described as] infectious toxic shock, then they died,"" she said.The test results will be ready in 1-2 days.--Communicated by:ProMED-mail<promed@promedmail.org>[Symptomatically more likely to be anthrax but we must await the laboratory results.For maps of Kazakhstan, go to <http://www.ephotopix.com/image/asia/kazakhstan_political_map.gif> and <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/53825>.
