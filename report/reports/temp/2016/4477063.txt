Biharprabha News, Asian News International (ANI) report [edited]<http://news.biharprabha.com/2016/09/scrub-typhus-claims-7-lives-in-shimla/>Shimla is in the grip of scrub typhus fever again and it has claimed the lives of 7 people until now.Scrub typhus is an acute, febrile infectious illness is caused by mites. In the past 9 months, 1500 samples have been collected at the Indira Gandhi Medical College [IGMC] and Hospital and over 250 have tested positive for the fever. The Medical Superintendent of IGMC said that since 2008, over 25 patients are dying every year due to scrub typhus. Several cases of scrub typhus have also been reported from Solan, Bilaspur, and Mandi districts of the state.--Communicated by:ProMED-mail from HealthMap Alerts<promed@promedmail.org>[Maps of India can be seen at <http://www.mapsofindia.com/maps/india/india-political-map.htm> and <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/304>.
