Thief River Falls Times [edited]<http://www.trftimes.com/news/mumps-outbreak-reported>As of 29 Mar 2016, a total of 16 confirmed and probable mumps cases were reported in Minnesota, according to the Minnesota Department of Health [MDH]. On average, fewer than 10 cases are reported each year in the state.In North Dakota, 14 cases have been reported since 24 Feb 2016. Most were in Ward County. On average, fewer than 3 cases are reported in North Dakota each year.
