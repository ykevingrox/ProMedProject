Public Health Agency of Sweden [in Swedish, machine trans., edited]<https://www.folkhalsomyndigheten.se/nyheter-och-press/nyhetsarkiv/2016/april/salmonellautbrott-utreds-korv-misstankt-orsak/>This spring, nearly 40 people in 11 different counties have suffered from gastrointestinal disorders caused by a certain type of _Salmonella_. The Public Health Agency is investigating the outbreak in collaboration with other relevant authorities.Public Health Agency microbial monitoring signaled at the beginning of April 2016 to a plurality of people [fell sick] with the same type of Salmonella. In interviews with the people who have been ill emerged a link to [a type of] sausage and therefore such products are now under investigation.Tulip Picante Salami Sticks brand GOL is one of the suspect products in the outbreak and was recalled on Friday, 22 Apr 2016, by the company. The recall was due to _Salmonella_ being detected in opened packages in homes of people with the illness.The Outbreak investigation [is being] conducted in close cooperation between the Public Health Agency , Food Agency, the National Veterinary Institute (SVA) , the infectious disease units and office environment .--Communicated by:ProMED-mail<promed@promedmail.org>[ProMED awaits the scope of this outbreak.
