Dunya News [edited]<http://dunyanews.tv/en/Pakistan/352436-One-more-patient-diagnosed-with-Congo-virus-in-Que>One more patient diagnosed with Congo virus [Crimean-Congo hemorrhagic fever] was admitted in Quetta's Fatima Jinnah Hospital [Balochistan province] on Wednesday [7 Sep 2016]. With the recent diagnosis, the number of Congo patients admitted in the hospital in the ongoing year has reached 97, among which 27 have been confirmed of having the virus. The rest of the patients have been discharged after recovery, whereas blood samples of 2 people have been sent to Karachi for testing. The death toll from the deadly virus is 10 in the provincial capital. A day before, 25-year-old LM was shifted to hospital over bleeding from nose and mouth. He is a resident of Eastern bypass and works in a dairy farm. Crimean-Congo hemorrhagic fever (CCHF) spreads through vectors that carry the virus found in cattle.
