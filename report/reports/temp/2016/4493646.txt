The death of 2 previously reported MERS cases was also reported.Details of the cases1. 39 year old male from Alkharj city, Riyadh Region developed symptoms on 11 Aug 2016 and was admitted to a hospital on 16 Aug 2016. A nasopharyngeal swab was collected on 17 Aug 2016 and tested positive for MERS-CoV on 18 Aug 2016. The case has no comorbid conditions. He had a history of contact with camels and consumption of their raw milk. There is no history of exposure to any of the other known risk factors. Currently the patient is in a stable condition admitted to a negative pressure room on a ward. The Ministry of Agriculture was informed and investigation of camels is ongoing. [Reported as a newly confirmed case on 18 Aug 2016, at which time he was noted to be in a stable condition. Currently the patient is in a stable condition admitted to a negative pressure room on a ward.
