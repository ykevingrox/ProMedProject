The News [edited]<http://thenewsnigeria.com.ng/2016/07/congo-launches-vaccination-campaign-after-85-die-from-yellow-fever/>[The DR] Congo on Wednesday [20 Jul 2016] launched a massive vaccination campaign against a yellow fever epidemic which has killed no fewer than 85 people, health officials said.The authorities have reported 1866 confirmed or suspected cases, including 85 deaths, since Jan [2016] in the provinces of the capital Kinshasa, Kongo Central and Kwango.Eugene Kabambi from the World Health Organization has said the aim is to vaccinate about 12 million people in the 3 provinces located near Angola, where the outbreak is believed to have started. Kabambi noted that about 2 million people have already been vaccinated in Kongo Central and Kinshasa.--Communicated by:ProMED-mail from HealthMap Alerts<promed@promedmail.org>[Although the number of reported suspected and confirmed yellow fever (YF) cases and fatalities in the DR Congo continues to grow, some progress to control it is being made. The above vaccination effort that was started yesterday [20 Jul 2016] will deliver 1 million doses. Vaccine availability continues to be a constraint in the DR Congo. Earlier reports indicated that the massive, 10 million dose, 3 province vaccination campaign was not slated to begin until sometime in August, when a new lot of vaccine is to arrive from Brazil.HealthMap/ProMED-mail map showing the location of the DR Congo and neighboring Angola, where the outbreak started, can be accessed at: <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/165>. - Mod.TYA HealthMap/ProMED-mail map can be accessed at: <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/194>.]
