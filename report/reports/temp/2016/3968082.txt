Oxitec and Piracicaba City Hall have expanded the ""Friendly _Aedes aegypti_ Project"" in Piracicaba, Brazil following strong results for controlling this mosquito population. In April 2015, releasing self-limiting mosquitoes whose offspring do not survive was started. By the end of the calendar year, results had already indicated a reduction in wild mosquito larvae by 82 percent. Oxitec's efficacy trials across Brazil, Panama and the Cayman Islands all resulted in a greater than 90 percent suppression of the wild _Ae. aegypti_ mosquito population.<http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/expansion-of-oxitecs-vector-control-solution-in-brazil-attacking-source-of-zika-virus-and-dengue-fever-after-positive-program-results-300206021.html>Colombia (Risaralda department). 8 Jan 2016.
