As of 24 Apr 2016, a total of 204 397 (73 percent) pupils were vaccinated out of 279 393 pupils with parents' consent. A total of 362 case reports of adverse event following immunization (AEFI) from the 3 regions where the Dengue School Based Immunization Program [took place] were received with one fatality reported in a person with congenital heart disease. The top 5 common reactions were fever, headache, dizziness, vomiting, and rash. As of 9 Apr 2016, a total of 33 658 suspected dengue cases were reported nationwide. Most of the cases were from Region IV-A with 5546 cases out of 33 658; Region III with 4518 cases; Region X with 3604 cases; and National Capital Region (NCR) with 2469 cases.Meanwhile, the health chief reiterated that while the vaccine is proven 90 percent effective in preventing the incidence of severe dengue, cleanliness is still the most fundamental solution against mosquito-borne diseases like dengue.<http://news.pia.gov.ph/article/view/2131461666549/doh-releases-latest-dengue-immunization-coverage>[A single fatality in more than 204 000 cases, and that due to a co-morbidity -- great safety record! - Mod.JWMaps of the Philippines can be seen at <http://www.charleskeng.com/images-map/philmap.jpg> and <http://healthmap.org/promed/p/158>. - Sr.Tech.Ed.MJ]Malaysia (Johor state). 6 May 2016. Health officials in Sri Lanka are reporting nearly 16 000 dengue fever cases between [January and April 2016], with 14 dengue related fatalities to date [1 May 2016]. Of the 15 502 cases reported in the 1st 4 months [2016], the capital area of Colombo district has recorded the most cases to date, accounting for about 1/3 of all cases (more than 5200), followed by the Gampaha, Jaffna, and Kalutara districts. Sri Lanka media report that the country is one of 10 which will participate in a dengue vaccine development study. DENVax is the 2nd potential dengue vaccine produced in the world, which is produced by the Takeda Pharmaceutical Company.The other countries which are part of the phase 3 of this multicentre clinical trial are Thailand, the Philippines, Viet Nam, Colombia, Brazil, Panama, the Dominican Republic, Nicaragua, and Peru. DENVax is said to offer protection against all 4 virus strains (serotypes) of dengue DEN-1, DEN-2, DEN-3, and DEN-4. Sanofi Pasteur's dengue vaccine, Dengvaxia, the world's first, has been approved by the governments of Mexico, the Philippines, Brazil, and El Salvador. Sudden report of 7 dengue cases in the past week has pushed the civic health department to take urgent steps in Kasba Bavda area [Kolhapur, Maharashtra] to control the breeding of the _Aedes aegypti_ mosquito, which [transmits] dengue. KMC [Kolhapur Municipal Corporation] officials said the blood reports of 7 patients from Kasba Bavda have tested positive for dengue. Last week [week of 25 Apr 2016], the state's government's malaria department also conducted tests and found 3 patients with dengue in the area.
