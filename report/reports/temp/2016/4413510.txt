Fiji is an island nation in Melanesia in the South Pacific Ocean about 2000 km (1300 miles) north east of New Zealand's North Island (<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fiji>). Fiji is divided into 4 major divisions: Central, Eastern, Northern, and Western. For a map of Fiji's divisions, see <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Fiji_divisions_named.png>. Fiji's wet season is from November to April, with the heaviest rains falling between December and mid-April (<http://www.lonelyplanet.com/fiji/weather>).
