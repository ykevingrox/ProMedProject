Young people who missed out on vaccinations because of the MMR scare have been urged to get immunised after an outbreak of measles among university students.There have been 8 confirmed cases of the disease in the Scottish capital [Edinburgh] in the past 2 weeks, with the majority being students at the University of Edinburgh. A pupil at James Gillespie's High School has also been confirmed as having the virus.Anyone not immunised is being urged by health bosses to act now to prevent further spread of the virus.
